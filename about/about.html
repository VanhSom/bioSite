About Sophia's Interests and Aspirations

Passion for Biology

Sophia is an individual driven by a deep passion for science, music, and exploration. With a passion for Biological Sciences, mainly Marine Biology and Zoology, she continually seeks opportunities to expand her knowledge and pursue her career aspirations. Alongside her scientific endeavors, she finds inspiration in music, actively participating in school choirs and orchestras for over seven years.

Her Love for Choir

A standout accomplishment for her was her involvement in the school's show choir program during her junior and senior years of high school. This experience introduced her to a lively community and instilled invaluable qualities such as resilience, perseverance, and confidence. Through rigorous rehearsals and performances, she honed her skills and emerged with newfound self-assurance.

Favorite Hobbies

In her leisure time, Sophia enjoys various hobbies, including visiting zoos and aquariums, traveling, exploring her cultural heritage, and going to see musicals. She has enjoyed productions such as Wicked, Aladdin, and The Lion King in theaters all across the Midwest. Her passion for biology has driven her since her first year of high school, igniting a desire to turn it into a career. She aspires to travel to Laos, her family's origin, to reconnect with her roots, immerse herself in the culture, and explore the diverse natural landscapes. Through these experiences, she finds inspiration and fulfillment, fueling her dedication and enthusiasm for her future endeavors.
