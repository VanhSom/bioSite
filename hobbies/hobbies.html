Hobbies

Visiting Zoos and Aquariums

Exploring zoos and aquariums is Sophia's cherished hobby, and the Omaha Zoo holds a special place in her heart. Located in Nebraska, it offers a sanctuary where she can marvel at wildlife from across the globe. The indoor aquarium is a standout feature, showcasing vibrant marine life in an enchanting undersea setting. Beyond the aquatic wonders, the zoo's exhibits highlight diverse terrestrial species, providing valuable insights into our planet's beauty. The zoo's emphasis on conservation and education resonates deeply with her, strengthening her dedication to preserving our natural world.

Choir

Music has been central to Sophia's life through school ensembles. Over seven years, she has dedicated herself to choir, winning grand champion twice with their show choir. As a member of Roosevelt Executive Suite and Roosevelt Capital Harmony, she has honed her skills and learned the value of dedication. The demands of show choir require extensive practice and commitment in rehearsals and competitions across different states. These experiences have shaped her journey, offering growth as a musician and team player. Winning grand champion multiple times showcases their collective passion for music and the power of teamwork.

Going to Musicals

One of Sophia's hobbies is going to watch musicals. She has seen Wicked, Aladdin, and The Lion King at the theaters in Omaha, Minneapolis, and Sioux Falls. She enjoys the live musical performances and the amazing production.
